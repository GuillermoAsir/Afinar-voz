How to Use

  • Choose your microphone, then press Start to grant access.
  • Sing a sustained note. The tuner shows the nearest musical note and how sharp/flat you are.
  • Use the A4 reference tone to match tuning (toggle on/off).
  • If the mic is blocked when opening from disk, open via localhost or HTTPS.
